The Invincibles

The “Invincibles” refers to the Arsenal F.C. 2003–04 team that went the entire Premier League season unbeaten. Under manager Arsène Wenger, Arsenal played 38 league games without a single loss (26 wins, 12 draws), becoming the first club in modern English top-flight history to achieve that.

The Invincibles

The “Invincibles” refers to the Arsenal F.C. 2003–04 team that went the entire Premier League season unbeaten. Under manager Arsène Wenger, Arsenal played 38 league games without a single loss (26 wins, 12 draws), becoming the first club in modern English top-flight history to achieve that.

Premier League after 22 Years

After winning the Premier League in 2004, Arsenal spent 22 years trying to return to the top of English football. Financial struggles after moving stadiums, inconsistent squads, injuries, and competition from wealthier rivals stopped them from winning the league again. Under Mikel Arteta, Arsenal rebuilt into contenders and finally ended the drought by winning the 2025–26 Premier League title.

Youngest Premier League Player

Max Dowman became one of the brightest young stars in football after breaking multiple Premier League records with Arsenal F.C. in 2026. At just 16 years old, he became the youngest player to start a Premier League match, the youngest goalscorer in league history, and the youngest player to win the Premier League title.
